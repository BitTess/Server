New to Learning Management Systems?

The ability to deliver training and learning has often been limited to select use cases, with most staff or students using emails, file share systems and ad-hoc meetings for training. GO1 offers a more intuitive and intelligent option that enables companies to deliver their material in a smarter way. Here are just some of GO1's features:

  • Intuitive for all users
  • E-Commerce to sell your courses
  • Advanced reporting
  • Cloud-based system
  • Deliver content securely
