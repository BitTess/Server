GO1 Training is perfect for

Training Organisations

Here are some of our standard RTO features:

Certificate Management

Courses and modules can be set up to generate custom certificates or letters of completion for users based on designated conditions.

Testing, Tracking & Results

Our inbuilt assessment and quiz engine makes it easy to create both simple and sophisticated assessment structures. This helps to ensure the veracity and integrity of your assessments.

Sophisticated Reporting including AVETMISS

GO1 provides a range of reports out of the box and makes it easy to filter, sort and create new default reports. Additionally we provide full support for USI and AVETMISS.

Use Your Domain

You can use your own domain or subdomain with GO1, ensuring your training is a seamless experience for your staff or customers.

E-Commerce & Website

GO1 makes it easy to sell your courses with a built in website with ecommerce. Already integrated with PayPal and other credit card processors plus use features such as coupons to maximise your signups!

Upload any Material

GO1 supports a range of files to make it easy to use your existing content. This covers SCORM, xAPI, PDF, video, Microsoft Office files (including DOCX and PPTX) and much more.
