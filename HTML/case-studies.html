Some of our enterprise clients


Corporate Education Solutions

ENTERPRISE LEARNING MANAGEMENT

GO1 LMS enables industry-specific learning outcomes and our customers include large corporates, government, not-for profit and registered training organisations. GO1's client base is truly global, with localised support in the United States, United Kingdom and Australia, where we offer training courses, live demostrations and face-to-face workshops.

Financial Services

ENTERPRISE LEARNING MANAGEMENT

One of the “Big Four” banks in Australia uses GO1 to improve the economic empowerment of women with a client-facing learning program. A social venture of the company’s Innovation department, the program is an online learning academy designed as a learning community for women across Australia.

Design for Change

EDUCARUNO, MEXICO

In Mexico, over 3,000 teachers use the Spanish version of GO1 to implement the global 'Design for Change' program - known locally as 'Diseña el Cambio'. Students in Mexico have created over 6600 social change projects countrywide. Learn more about the project at go1.com

Leadership development

QUEENSLAND EDUCATION LEADERSHIP INSTITUTE

QELi is an innovative and landmark venture to equip school leaders with the skills and training they need to help drive major education reforms. The organisation provides onsite and elearning training across Australia and Asia, with key partnerships across local and international tertiary institutions (including Harvard), government and other education bodies.

Global Training

GIRL GEEK ACADEMY

As seen in Fast Company, CNet and Gizmodo, Girl Geek Academy are using GO1 to teach over one million women how to build apps and create startups by 2025. From New York to Poland to Sydney, Girl Geek Academy uses GO1 to bring together this community of women keen to adopt new tech skills from their peers.
